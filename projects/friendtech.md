# Friend.tech

Category: Social  
Type: Tokenized Social Platform
Overview:
Friend.tech enables users to tokenize access to private chat groups.
The model combines social networking with financial incentives.
## Economic Model
Users buy and sell "keys" representing access.
Prices adjust algorithmically based on demand.
## Adoption Considerations
Adoption depends on:
- Influencer participation
- Network effects
- Platform trust
## Competitive Landscape
Competes with:
- Uniswap
- Other Base-native DEXs
Liquidity incentives determine long-term dominance.
